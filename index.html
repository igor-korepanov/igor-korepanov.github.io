My research spans the following eight directions:

  • Yang--Baxter equation and algebraic curves
  • Tetrahedron equation
  • Geometric infinitesimal relations imitating Pachner moves in two, three and four dimensions, and related stories
  • Polygon relations in Grassmann algebras
  • Polygon relations with usual commuting variables and related algebraic structures
  • Miscellaneous algebraic
  • Simplex and polygon cocycles/cohomology
  • Self-similarity on cubic lattices

  1. Вакуумные кривые L-операторов, связанных с шестивершинной моделью, и построение R-операторов

    Comment: This and the next paper were the first ones where Yang--Baxter solutions were discovered related to algebraic curves of genus > 1.
    Later, in 1997, two other people, whose names are not worth mentioning here, re-discovered them in their own way, and were very unhappy with my priority. For some time, they used to publish papers about the "history" of this subject, where my papers were mentioned in a footnote but not in the list of references!
    VINITI is Russian Institute for Scientific and Technical Information. My scientific advisor Felix Berezin had died tragically, I myself was inexperienced, so I had no access to scientific journals at that time.
